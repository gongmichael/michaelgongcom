Economic Significance evaluation

I setup a option straddle strategy to evaluate the economic significance of DFM and DFMX models. Specifically: * A buy (sell) signal is generated if the forecasted implied volatility increase (decrease) by more than 1% (-1%). * The least net cost of portfolio is set to 1$. * No risk free rate is assumed (for days without trading, simply zero returns).
